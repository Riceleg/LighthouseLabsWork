Banana Oatmeal Cookie Recipe

Ingredients
  • 2 large old bananas
  • 1 cup of oats (quick or regular, not whole - chopped)
  • Plus something yummy i.e. chocolate chips
Directions
  1. Mix everything up!
  2. On a greased cookie sheet place even cookie sized dollups
  3. Cook them at 350 degrees for 15 minutes
